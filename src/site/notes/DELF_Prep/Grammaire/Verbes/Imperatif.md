# 🗣️ L'Impératif (The Imperative)

The imperative is used to give **orders**, **advice**, or **requests**. It only exists for three persons: **Tu**, **Nous**, and **Vous**.

## 1. How to form it?
It is based on the **Present Tense** conjugation, but the subject pronoun (*Tu, Nous, Vous*) is **removed**.

### 🧱 Regular Verbs
| Subject | Formation Rule | Example (Parler) | Example (Finir) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tu** | Present form, **drop the -s** for -ER verbs | **Parle !** [paʁl] | **Finis !** [fini] |
| **Nous** | Same as Present | **Parlons !** [paʁlɔ̃] | **Finissons !** [finisɔ̃] |
| **Vous** | Same as Present | **Parlez !** [paʁle] | **Finissez !** [finise] |

---

## 2. Irregular Verbs (The Big 4)
These are very common in directions and requests.

| Verb | Tu | Nous | Vous |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Être** (to be) | **Sois** [swa] | **Soyons** [swajɔ̃] | **Soyez** [swaje] |
| **Avoir** (to have) | **Aie** [ɛ] | **Ayons** [ɛjɔ̃] | **Ayez** [ɛje] |
| **Savoir** (to know) | **Sache** [saʃ] | **Sachons** [saʃɔ̃] | **Sachez** [saʃe] |
| **Vouloir** (to want) | **Veuille** [vœj] | - | **Veuillez** [vœje] |

---

## 3. Practical Examples for A2
- **Giving directions:** \"**Tourne** à gauche !\" [tuʁn a ɡoʃ] (Turn left!)
- **Making a request:** \"**Veuillez** patienter, s'il vous plaît.\" [vœje pasjɑ̃te] (Please wait).
- **Encouragement:** \"**Soyons** courageux !\" [swajɔ̃ kuʁaʒø] (Let's be brave!).
